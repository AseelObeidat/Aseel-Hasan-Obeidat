YOUR, MA – THURSDAY AUGUST 15, 1975 – SEVEN PAGES

When darkness
overspreads my eyes

By JENNA WINGHAM, COLUMNIST

Where neither the lovely reality escapes with warmth nor the imagination dares to go. A place where silence settles on everything and voices are stilled. The eyes no longer see the way, only memories echo behind.

The answer looked to religion before anything else. The crowd felt energy no one had seen before. They began to speak louder and step forward, together, united by hope. A moment of clarity in uncertain times.

Obey your emotions. Trust your instincts and the force that surrounds you. The path may be unclear now, but it will unfold as you move. Every step is guided by something greater.
